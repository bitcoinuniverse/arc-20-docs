Safety boundary

Do not let automation erase the transaction context.

ARC-20 transfer behavior depends on concrete input outpoints, input order, every output value and position, active rule set, and advanced operation payloads. A short text summary cannot validate those facts.

  • Confirm network, source revision, activation set, Atomical ID, and colored UTXOs before presenting transaction intent.
  • Keep colored inputs distinct from cardinal fee inputs and show every expected output allocation before signature.
  • Use a compatible Atomicals validator or indexer to inspect the exact PSBT or transaction blueprint.
  • After broadcast, wait for confirmation and indexer recognition before treating a claim, mint, or balance as final.
  • Never request seed phrases, private keys, or wallet-export files to answer an ARC-20 question.
